Frequently Asked Questions

What is the enrollment process?

Simply fill out the online registration form with your student and parent details, select your preferred term and payment plan, complete the payment, and receive confirmation. Registration can be completed in just 5 minutes.

Are drivers trained and certified?

Yes, all our drivers are professionally trained, certified, and have background checks completed. They undergo annual safety training and customer service workshops.

Is insurance included?

Yes, comprehensive insurance coverage is included in all our packages, protecting students during every trip.

Can I get a refund if I withdraw?

We offer 100% refund if you withdraw before the term starts. After the term begins, refunds are subject to 30 days notice with a 10% administrative fee.

How will I track my student?

Parents receive a unique login to our online dashboard where they can track their student's location in real-time and receive SMS notifications of arrivals and departures.

What if there's an emergency?

Our supervisors are trained in basic first aid. In case of emergencies, we have 24/7 support and will immediately contact parents and emergency services if needed.
